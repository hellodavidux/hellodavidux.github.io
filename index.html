About me

I studied industrial design engineering and began with physical products—designing air fresheners and lamps, and repairing 3D printers.

I later brought the same systems thinking, principles, and craft to digital services.

I'm a digital product specialist. I've worked across AI enterprise SaaS, B2B services, sports media, iGaming, affiliate marketing, and e-commerce.

Outside work, I coach tennis, play football, and padel, and follow Real Madrid (yes, even though I'm from Barcelona). I'm drawn to urban mobility, utopian history, and behavioural psychology—especially nudging—and Growth.Design has long been an inspiration.
